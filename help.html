Frequently Asked Questions



How do I get started with the platform?

Simply create an account using your email address, and you'll be guided through our onboarding process to set up your first project.


Is my data secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and blockchain-based security protocols to ensure that your information remains private and tamper-proof.


How can I reset my password?

Go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." We will send a reset link to your registered email address immediately.


Where can I find my project documentation?

All documentation is available in the 'Resources' tab of your dashboard, or you can check our GitHub repository for technical specs.
